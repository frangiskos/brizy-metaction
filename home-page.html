metACTION

Semi-independent Accommodation Facilities for Unaccompanied Minors

Welcome to metACTION website

metACTION is a non-profit organization that provides housing and psycho-social support services to unaccompanied minors. It is active in social and humanitarian issues and its goal is to create and implement programs through humanitarian action and the provision of social care. It also aims to maintain social cohesion through the implementation of actions that will help to reduce social inequalities, create equal opportunities and prevent social exclusion of vulnerable groups of the population.

More specifically, the main objective of metACTION is the operation of semi-independent Accommodation Facilities for unaccompanied minors by ensuring an environment of protection, care and their smooth integration into society.
